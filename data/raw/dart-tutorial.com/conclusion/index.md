## Congratulations

Congratulations on completing the Dart course. Apply your newfound knowledge toward building applications with Flutter and revisit the material whenever needed for reference support.

## Next Steps: Learning Flutter

After mastering Dart fundamentals, the recommended pathway is to explore Flutter, described as the most popular app development framework. This technology enables developers to create high-quality applications across multiple platforms from a unified codebase.

### Why Flutter?

- Single codebase supports Android, iOS, Web, and Desktop platforms
- Extensive community backing and resources
- Accessible learning curve for beginners
- Built on Dart as its programming language
